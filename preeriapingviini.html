Preeriapingviini

I was involved in the website redesign for my current employer, Preeriapingviini. My first task was to create a layout model of the redesigned website, which required me to benchmark other companies from the design industry and consider changes to the content hierarchy of the website. I created a prototype of the upcoming website in Figma that included templates for the most common page types and navigation elements on the site. Its purpose was to work as a platform where different ideas could be tested before implementation.

After this, I participated in the website's WordPress development in a supporting role, mostly helping in updating content on the site and writing CSS. Accessibility was a key component of the development process. The website was built with WCAG standards in mind and later inspected with Axe DevTools to ensure web accessibility requirements were met.
