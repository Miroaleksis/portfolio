The task was to redesign Lova's website, mostly in terms of appearance, content hierarchy and accessibility. My role was to prototype a layout model of the new website in Figma, in which different ideas and customers' wishes could be tested with low threshold before actual implementation of the website. The website was updated with Lova's visual identity in mind, which included logo, color scheme and fonts among other things. Also, the contents of the website remained roughly the same, only their location and hierarchy were affected.

I also participated in the website's WordPress development, updating contents to the site and writing CSS. Accessibility was a key component of the development process. The website was built with WCAG standards in mind and later inspected with Axe DevTools to ensure web accessibility requirements were met.
