Graphic design

Staffroom previously operated under a different business name, and due to the transition to the current one, I was asked to design a new corporate look and visual identity for the company. I therefore designed a new logo, as well as defined new fonts, colors, and other brand elements for the client. At the client's request, I created two logo options that they could use as they saw fit depending on the situation. After that, I produced various commissioned works for the client, such as banners and flyers over the course of a few years.
