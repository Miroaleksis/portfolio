About Me

I am a UX & UI designer from Helsinki, with a background in graphic design. I am proficient in various user research, conceptualization, and usability testing methods, as well as the implementation of interactive prototypes. As a graphic designer, I have extensive experience ranging from creating corporate identities to designing publication materials. I have also frequently been involved in frontend development for websites.

I have recently started working as an accessibility specialist, a role that involves conducting accessibility audits for various public sector entities. I am a CPACC-certified member of the International Association of Accessibility Professionals (IAAP), and I am accustomed to prioritizing accessibility in design.

I originally graduated from Pekka Halonen Academy in 2018 as a visual artisan, competence area in graphic design.
